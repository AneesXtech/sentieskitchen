Our Story

Meet Sentie
Kiondo

Sentie “Shen” Kiondo is a proud, talented Ugandan chef whose journey began at the intersection of culture, community, and a deep love for food.

Inspired by his roots and the rich culinary traditions of Uganda, Sentie built Sentie’s Kitchen to share authentic flavors while creating welcoming spaces where people come together.

With years of experience and a heart for service, Chef Sentie continues to honor his heritage while serving his community with passion, creativity, and unforgettable food.
